// Features
Everything a contractor needs

From the first nail to the final invoice — manage your entire business from your iPhone.

📁
Project Management

Track budgets, monitor financial summaries, and get color-coded budget utilization alerts at 80% and 100%.

💸
Expense Tracking

Log expenses by category — Materials, Labor, Equipment, Miscellaneous — with advanced filtering and search.

🧾
Invoice Management

Create and track invoices with Paid, Pending, and Overdue statuses. Automatic overdue detection included.

👥
Client Directory

Maintain a full client database with contact details. Auto-create clients when adding new projects or invoices.

👷
Labor & Workers

Register workers with Hourly, Daily, Contract, or Subcontractor types. Dual pay rates with automatic calculations.

📊
Financial Analytics

Full dashboard with donut charts, trend lines, category breakdowns, top projects, and budget utilization — powered by Swift Charts.

📷
OCR Invoice Scanning

Scan receipts with your camera. On-device OCR extracts amount, date, and description automatically — no internet needed.

☁️
iCloud Sync

All your data syncs automatically across all your Apple devices via CloudKit. Works even for deletions, instantly.

🔔
Smart Notifications

Get reminders for invoice due dates, overdue alerts, and budget warnings. Tap to navigate directly to the relevant item.
