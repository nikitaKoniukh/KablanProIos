Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my Pro subscription after reinstalling?
Open the app → go to Settings → tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
My data isn't syncing across devices. What should I do?
Make sure iCloud Drive is enabled on both devices: iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → iCloud Drive. Then open Settings inside KablanPro and tap "Sync Now". If the issue persists, sign out of iCloud and back in.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → KablanPro → Cancel Subscription. Your Pro access continues until the end of the current billing period.
Can I export my data?
Yes. Go to Settings → Export Data. You can export everything as a JSON file, or export individual project reports as formatted text via the iOS share sheet.
The OCR scanner didn't extract the correct amount. What can I do?
After scanning, a review screen lets you manually edit all extracted fields (amount, date, description) before saving. OCR works best on clear, well-lit photos of printed receipts.
How do I change the language or currency?
Go to Settings inside the app. You can switch between English, Hebrew, and Russian instantly. Currency can also be changed from the same Settings screen.
I hit the free tier limit. What are my options?
The free tier supports 1 project and 2 workers. Upgrade to Pro (Monthly or Yearly) from the paywall to unlock unlimited projects and workers.
Is my data safe? Does the app send data to any server?
No. All data is stored on your device and optionally in your personal iCloud account. We have no access to your data and use no third-party analytics or tracking. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

App Requirements

iOS: 17.0 or later

Device: iPhone (iPad not currently supported)

iCloud Sync: Requires iCloud account with iCloud Drive enabled

Notifications: Requires notification permission (optional)

Camera/Photos: Required only for OCR invoice scanning (optional)
